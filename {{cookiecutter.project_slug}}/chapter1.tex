\chapter{Introduction}
\label{chap:intro}
This thesis explores fundamental concepts in optimization theory and their applications. 
The field of convex optimization has seen significant development since the foundational work of Rockafellar~\cite{rockafellar1970convex}. 
More recently, Boyd and Vandenberghe~\cite{boyd2004convex} have provided a comprehensive modern treatment that bridges theory and practice.

    \text{subject to} & g(x) \le 0.

\end{equation}

    \toprule
    $A$ & $B$\\

    % the csv file has names colnameA, colnameB
